Profit / Loss Trade Calculator Explained

This tool will help traders on Bitfinex.com to calculate profits or losses on long postions (= buy coins sell coins). By specifying your maker and taker fees, the number of coins and the price to open a position, the calculator outputs a Break Even Price (the price to close the position without profit/loss after paying fees) and tables displaying possible profit / loss when closing the position at the given price.

The line on top will output:
Break Even Price: the price to close the position without profit/loss after paying opening and closing fees
Buy Amount (USD): the total USD amount to open the position (including the fees to open the position)
Buy Fee: the fees payed for opening the position

The Profit and loss Tables show:
Sell @: the price to close the position at
Price Diff.: the USD difference between the buy price and the sell price
Profit USD: the profit of the trade in USD after paying opening and closing fees
Profit %: the profit of the trade in percentage after paying opening and closing fees
Order Return: the total amount in USD returned to your accountâ€™s balance after closing the position
Sell Fee: the fees payed for closing the position
