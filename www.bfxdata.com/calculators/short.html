Profit / Loss Trade Calculator Explained

This tool will help traders on Bitfinex.com to calculate profits or losses on short postions (= sell coins buy coins). By specifying your maker and taker fees, the number of coins and the price to open a position, the calculator outputs a Break Even Price (the price to close the position without any profit / loss after paying fees) and tables displaying possible profit / loss when closing the position at the given price.

The line on top will output:
Break Even Price: the price to close the position without profit/loss after paying opening and closing fees
Sell Amount (USD): the total USD amount to open the position (including the fees to open the position)
Sell Fee: the fees payed for opening the position

The Profit and loss Tables show:
Buy @: the price to close the position at
Price Diff.: the USD difference between the sell price and the buy price
Profit USD: the profit of the trade in USD after paying opening and closing fees
Profit %: the profit of the trade in percentage after paying opening and closing fees
Buy Fee: the fees payed for closing the position
